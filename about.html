Yael Levey is a photographer who loves to travel and take beautiful pictures that capture the indefinable beauty in our surroundings.

Yael lives and works in London but mostly takes inspiration for her photos from travelling on a shoestring around the world.

Some of the favourite places Yael has been to include Brazil, Jamaica, the Grand Canyon, Morocco and Jordan.

Yael Levey Photos is on Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest.
